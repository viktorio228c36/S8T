About us

“C8-Trading” company produces high-quality charcoal from hardwood (oak, hornbeam, ash). For many years our products have been providing EU countries, as well as Romania, Poland, Germany, France, Belgium, Latvia, Lithuania, Estonia, United Kingdom and other countries in the world. Environmental friendliness, high quality, flawless reputation, innovations and constant improvement – our core principal of work and our companies` mission.

Products

Equipment

Our charcoal is produced using specialized, factory equipment. Due to its physico-chemical properties charcoal is not only used for grilling, it is also of great importance for chemistry and metallurgy.
Buying our charcoal, you get:
- charcoal that corresponds to the GOST-7657-84 and DIN EN 1860-2:2005 standard;
- shipping to any country in Europe;
- flexible payment forms.

Charcoal characteristics

Charcoal corresponds to the A-Mark, GOST -7657-84 and DIN EN 1860-2:2005
carbon content - 85-90%,
ash content -2-5%,
humidity – 1-3%.
Product features:
- smokeless;
- scentless;
- no chimical additives;
- with high calorific value;
- flammable;
- long burning time.

Charcoal packaging options

Charcoal can be packaged into big-bags, polypropylene bags, 13-15 weight, as well as paper bags of different weight and size for customer’s demand.
Our company has been collaborating with trustworthy and reliable paper bag manufacturers, that is why we can package our products into our producer’s bags, or into bags provided by our customer.
Our equipment allows us to package around a ton an hour and well-timed sorting system guarantees products high quality.
